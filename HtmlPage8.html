About Us - Collect and Donate

Welcome to Collect and Donate, where our mission is to make a positive impact on the world by collecting resources and donations to support those in need. We believe that together, we can create meaningful change and improve the lives of individuals and communities.

Our Vision

At Collect and Donate, we envision a world where generosity knows no bounds. We strive to build a community of compassionate individuals who come together to contribute resources, time, and support for the betterment of society.

What We Do

Our organization is dedicated to collecting various resources, including but not limited to home items, supplies, and volunteer efforts. We collaborate with like-minded individuals, businesses, and institutions to make a collective impact on issues of resuable items goes in trash and environmental sustainability.

How You Can Help

We believe that every contribution, no matter how small, can make a difference. You can join us in our mission by:

  • Donating things to support our initiatives.
  • Providing in-kind donations such as clothing, Furniture, educational, toys, electrical materials in working condition.
  • Volunteering your time and skills to help with our projects and programs.

Charges

While this service is offered at no cost for donations and collections, a nominal fee of 1 pound for each item will be charged for confirmation and to sustain its operation.
